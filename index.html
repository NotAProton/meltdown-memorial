OWASP MELTDOWN

An Interactive Cryptography Challenge

February 27, 2026

THE EVENT

On February 27th, 2026, the OWASP Chapter of IIIT Kottayam hosted an interactive cryptography challenge. Students from all years competed in head-to-head teams (1-3 players) in a thrilling game where they either defended a reactor from meltdown or orchestrated chaos as saboteurs.

THE GAME

Meltdown is a real-time cryptographic challenge game featuring two asymmetric roles:

CONTROLLER

Tasked with protecting the reactor by submitting commands that must pass a cipher challenge without triggering sabotage mechanisms.

SABOTEUR

Attempts to trigger a meltdown by breaking ciphers in a limited window, forcing incorrect decisions on the controller.

OWASP IIIT KOTTAYAM

The OWASP (Open Worldwide Application Security Project) Chapter at IIIT Kottayam dedicates itself to advancing cybersecurity education and awareness among students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, fostering a culture of security-first thinking.

Meltdown was our inaugural event.

OPEN SOURCE

The Meltdown game engine has been released as open-source software to inspire future events and adaptations. Explore the full source code, extend the cipher library, and build your own security challenges.
